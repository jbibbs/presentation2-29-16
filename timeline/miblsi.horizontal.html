  1. Project Brief approved

    January 23rd, 2014

    The project brief was read and approved. It contained details on how the project would move forward. The information architecture process was set to begin.

  2. Sitemap developed with rough content outline

    January 5th, 2015

    Almost a year after the project kickoff an initial draft of the sitemap was in place with early content developed around it.

  3. MIBLSI Logo approved

    May 8th, 2015

    The logo contest was complete and a final logo was chosen. After many revisions, the final logo was in place.

  4. User interviews conducted

    July 9th, 2015

    Due to concerns with the proposed sitemap, user interviews at local schools were conducted to help validate the structure and ensure we were on the right course.

  5. Final Sitemap Approval

    November 20th, 2015

    After many revisions and incorporating user feedback, a final sitemap was developed and approved.

  6. Development Groundwork Begins

    December 1st, 2015

    The server was set up and initial work on developing the website had begun.

  7. Wireframes Aproved

    December 31st, 2015

    The wireframes (blueprints) for the website were developed and approved.

  8. Mockups Approved

    January 31st, 2016

    Full color mockups (graphic designs) for the website were developed and approved.

  9. All Features Reviewed and Approved

    March 28th, 2016

    All of the outstanding features (Training materials system, Event system, Presentation archiving) have been reviewed and approved.

  10. CMS & Governance Training

    May 28th, 2016

    Training on how to use the content management system and keep information up-to-date over time.

  11. Website Launch!

    May 31st, 2016

    Thanks everyone! Huzzah!
